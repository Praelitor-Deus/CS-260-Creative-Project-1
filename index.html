Welcome to BYU Quark!

We foster a sense of community among those who have a mutual interest in the genres of science-fiction and fantasy, in all their many forms. We hold regular socials and film forums, as well a run a book club, a writing group, gaming events, and art meetings. We have an insanely active online forum where there's great discussion. If you have any interest in sci-fi or fantasy, this is your club!
