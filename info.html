Our goal is to foster a sense of community among those who have a mutual interest in the genres of science-fiction and fantasy in any form. Books, Art, Videogames or TTRPG's are all welcome! We hold regular socials and film forums, as well a run a book club, a writing group, gaming events, and art meetings. Our online forum is active and full of information! If you have any interest in sci-fi or fantasy, this is your club!
